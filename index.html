All about Me

My name is Vance Weston

Growing up in Laketown, Utah, I never really had much of an experience with big towns, computers, shopping malls, or freeways. Any little bit of research would show you that Laketown, sitting on the sourthern shore of Bear Lake, has a population of 250-300 people depending on the year. With this kind of demographic population I grew up on a cattle ranch.

Wrestling cattle, branding, riding horses, and driving tractors were my skills as a boy. As these things may not translate into a powerful career in the world today, they have helped sculpt me into a successful employee, student, and person. I learned hard work and diligence, which helped me in the many fields I have been in, but currently they help me as I have been studying and applying Web Development.
